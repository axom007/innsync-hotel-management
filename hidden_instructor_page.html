Customer Profile

InnSync serves small to mid-sized hotels where owners and on-ground managers want fast, practical wins without complex software rollouts. The focus is on plugging revenue leaks, improving visibility of bookings and occupancy, and keeping day-to-day operations simple.

Primary Stakeholders

  • Hotel Owners ‚Äì Traditional, ROI-first decision-makers who value clear numbers and quick outcomes.
  • Hotel Managers ‚Äì Time-pressed operators who need speed, clarity, and minimal friction in daily workflows.

Key Traits & Preferences

  • Action-oriented: Prefer a direct phone call over long forms; expect quick answers.
  • Language comfort: Appreciate choosing a preferred language upfront for clarity and trust.
  • Pragmatic: Care about measurable gains (e.g., +10% revenue uplift) more than flashy features.

Jobs To Be Done

  • Track real-time occupancy and bookings across channels.
  • Detect and prevent unrecorded or missed bookings that leak revenue.
  • Standardize simple, reliable front-desk processes.

Decision Triggers

  • Evidence of lost revenue or mismatch between stays and records.
  • Manual/spreadsheet processes slowing the front desk.
  • Need for lightweight tools that work well on mobile.

Why InnSync Fits

  • Measurable outcome first: Designed to surface and close revenue gaps.
  • Simple onboarding: Minimal setup; optimized for quick manager adoption.
  • Owner-friendly narrative: Clear numbers, crisp copy, and a prominent call-to-call CTA.
