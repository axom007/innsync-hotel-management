Design Philosophy

  • Purposeful color strategy – We chose orange to convey energy, attention, and optimism, balanced with cream/off-white for readability. This reflects warmth, confidence, and clarity.
  • Light & Dark Mode – Supports user comfort in all environments, improves accessibility, and saves battery on OLED displays.
  • FAQ Section for Clarity & Trust – Proactively addresses user doubts, building authority and supporting conversions.
  • Minimal, repeat-focused landing – Short layout ensures key CTAs remain visible, reducing decision fatigue and bounce rates.
  • Demo-first conversion funnel – Our priority is a compelling, interactive demo that converts leads into customers.
  • No heavy media, just clarity – Hotel owners already understand the problem; removing heavy media boosts speed and accessibility.
