InnSync PMS • About Us

Building hospitality that runs on truth

InnSync PMS helps hotels stop revenue leakages and capture every rupee. We combine a modern PMS with IoT-verified occupancy, instant alerts, and simple workflows your team will actually enjoy using. Our mission is to bring operational truth to hospitality so owners grow profitably while guests get a consistently great stay.

Hospitality Industry is one of the largest employers and contributors to India's GDP. Despite many innovations in the industry, there are still many revenue leakages. We at InnSync build solutions to stop these revenue leakages. The primary product that we are working on is an automated room occupancy detector, giving the hotel owner real-time data of room occupancy, striking down any possibility of pilferage.
